Purpose

Our commitment is to support and represent, before the three orders of Government, the Commerce, Services and Tourism sectors through advice, management, and defense of their interests and activities derived from their own nature, seeking the creation of strategic alliances that generate the appropriate conditions for sustainable economic development that promotes competitiveness through the improvement of quality and service for the benefit not only of our partners but also of the Community.
